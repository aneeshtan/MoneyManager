Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

Pro Money Manager is designed as a private, local-first personal finance app.

Data Collection

Pro Money Manager does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal data. The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, third-party account aggregation, or cloud sync.

Data Stored on Device

The app stores finance records you create or import, including accounts, categories, budgets, merchant rules, imported transaction text, and backup/export history. This data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData.

Imported PDF, CSV, and pasted message content is processed on device. The app does not upload these files or their contents to a server.

Exports and Backups

You may choose to export CSV or JSON backup files. Exported files are created only after you request them and are controlled by you through iOS sharing and file storage features.

Tracking

Pro Money Manager does not track you across apps or websites.
